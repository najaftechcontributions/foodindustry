About Us

We Are Food Industry Asia

Leading the Future of Food & Beverage in Asia Pacific

Who We Are

Food Industry Asia is a Singapore-based, non-profit membership association uniting the region’s leading food and beverage brands to advance science-based policy, sustainability, and growth.

Our Mission

To represent the food and beverage industry in Asia – promoting a climate for sustainable growth and serving as a regional knowledge hub for science-based advocacy.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant food and beverage industry for a healthy and prosperous Asia.
