WHY JOIN FIA

Strengthen Your Regional Influence

As a member of Food Industry Asia (FIA), you gain access to exclusive insights, advocacy platforms, and collaboration opportunities that strengthen your organisation's ability to operate, influence, and grow across the region.

FIA membership connects you directly to decision-makers, regulators, and industry peers, positioning your company at the heart of Asia's most critical food system discussions.

Join a trusted network of regional and multinational companies driving sustainable growth, advancing science-based policy, and leading innovation across Asia's food and beverage sector.

MEMBERSHIP PILLARS

How FIA Supports Your Business

Three core pillars guide our work and deliver value to every member

Regulatory Affairs

Navigating Asia's complex regulatory landscape with expert support and real-time intelligence

  • Receive tailored guidance and analysis to help your business navigate regulatory and compliance systems across diverse Asian markets.
  • Access FIA's exclusive, members-only Issues Hub, a central repository of timely intelligence, in-depth analysis, and updates on emerging policies.
  • Engage directly with governments and regulatory bodies through FIA's coordinated channels and country-level working groups.

Public Affairs

Driving proactive engagement on nutrition, sustainability, and food safety policy

  • Gain early insights into evolving policy priorities, proposals, and regulatory trends that impact your business.
  • Participate in FIA-led initiatives that shape regional positions on health, nutrition, trade, and sustainability.
  • Leverage our experts' analysis and advocacy network to influence policy outcomes in key markets.

Regional Executives

Connecting industry leaders to drive collaboration and policy dialogue

  • Collaborate with the FIA Coordinating Council, made up of CEOs, Presidents, and senior executives from leading food and beverage companies.
  • Access exclusive executive-level roundtables, peer engagement sessions, and opportunities to meet senior government officials.
  • Help shape FIA's strategy and the broader direction of the food and beverage industry in Asia.
