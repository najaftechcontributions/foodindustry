Get to Know Food Industry Asia

Leading the Future of Food & Beverage in Asia Pacific

WHO WE ARE

Food Industry Asia

Food Industry Asia (FIA) is a Singapore-based, non-profit membership association established in 2010 by a consortium of leading multinational food and beverage companies.

We represent the region's most influential brands, ingredient suppliers, and innovators, serving as the collective voice of the food and beverage industry in Asia Pacific.

Through science-based advocacy, policy engagement, and multi-stakeholder collaboration, FIA works to create a thriving, sustainable, and resilient food system for the region.

OUR WORK

What We Do

Driving Impact Through Advocacy, Collaboration, and Leadership

Policy & Advocacy

FIA engages with governments, regulators, and industry partners to shape policies that enable sustainable growth, fair trade, and innovation in the food and beverage sector. We champion science-based regulation that protects consumers, supports business competitiveness, and advances public health outcomes.

Industry Collaboration

As a trusted convener, FIA connects global and regional companies with policymakers, research institutions, and local industry groups to address shared challenges — from food safety to sustainability — and to drive collective impact across markets.

Knowledge & Leadership

FIA serves as a regional hub for knowledge, insights, and best practices, empowering members to anticipate trends, navigate regulatory shifts, and lead responsibly in an evolving food landscape.

OUR PRIORITIES

Our Strategic Focus Areas

FIA's work is guided by three strategic focus areas that reflect the priorities shaping Asia's food system. Each area connects cross-market projects and collaborative initiatives that drive real impact for our members and stakeholders.

01

Health, Nutrition & Innovation

FIA promotes science-based approaches to improving health and nutrition across Asia. We work with regulators, public health experts, and industry partners to support responsible product innovation, reformulation, and clear nutrition communication. Our goal is to build trust and deliver better dietary outcomes for consumers in the region.

02

Smart Regulation, Trade & Safe Food

FIA supports the development of interoperable regulatory systems that strengthen regional trade and food safety. We advocate for Codex-aligned standards and practical policy frameworks that enable innovation, efficient compliance, and consumer protection across diverse markets.

03

Sustainability & Resilience

FIA helps members advance their sustainability commitments through collaboration and shared solutions. We focus on packaging waste management, Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR), and resource efficiency to promote responsible production and a more circular food economy in Asia.

OUR LEADERSHIP

FIA Coordinating Council

The FIA Coordinating Council comprises the region's most senior business leaders, guiding FIA's mission to promote sustainable growth and advance progressive, science-based policies across Asia Pacific. Their leadership ensures FIA remains a credible, future-focused voice for the food and beverage sector.

Guiding Our Mission Forward

Our Coordinating Council brings together decades of industry experience and regional expertise to steer FIA's strategic direction. These leaders represent the diversity and innovation of Asia's food and beverage landscape, ensuring our advocacy and initiatives reflect the real needs of our members and the communities they serve.
